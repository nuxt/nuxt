# E4007

Your project has files in `layouts/`, but `<NuxtLayout>` is not present in `app.vue`. Without it, your layouts have no effect.

## Resolution

Wrap `<NuxtPage>` with `<NuxtLayout>` in `app.vue`:

```vue
<template>
  <NuxtLayout>
    <NuxtPage />
  </NuxtLayout>
</template>
```
